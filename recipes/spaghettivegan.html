Vegan Spaghetti

Description

Quick and easy vegetable spaghetti that tastes so good, you won't even realize it’s loaded with nutritious veggies. This vegetarian pasta recipe has over 1 ½ pounds of vegetables. This spaghetti is so good, we could eat it every day.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Vegetables
  • Onion, garlic and tomato paste
  • Canned whole tomatoes
  • Herbs and spices
  • Mushroom powder

Instructions

  1. Soften chopped onions in olive oil over medium heat
  2. Stir in garlic, spices and tomato paste
  3. Throw in your vegetablesadn cook until tender
  4. Add canned whole tomatoes and break them up a bit with a spoon. You wantthe sauce to be chunky.
  5. Season and then simmer the sauce until it's reduced slightly --somewhere between 10 and 15 minutes.
  6. While the sauce simmers, cook your pasta.
  7. Toss the pasta with the sauce and serve.
